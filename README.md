# Tammad

## English

Tammad brings Zammad to where work often happens today: your iPhone and iPad. It is made for people who want to stay on top of their tickets while away from their desk, reply quickly, and avoid opening the browser for every small task.

Tammad is designed to feel like a true iOS app: clear, fast, calm, and focused on what matters most.

**App Store:** [Download Tammad](https://apps.apple.com/at/app/tammad/id6755873489)

### What Tammad Does

With Tammad, you can manage your Zammad tickets on the go:

- View, create, and update tickets
- Read and reply to articles
- Display HTML content and attachments cleanly
- Find the right ticket quickly with search
- Keep track of unread notifications

### Why People Like Tammad

- Native on iOS: built for fast, fluid interaction
- Focused: centered on what matters in day-to-day support work
- Secure: credentials are stored safely in the iOS Keychain
- Comfortable to use: optimized for both Light Mode and Dark Mode

### Who It Is For

Tammad is ideal for teams and individuals who use Zammad and want to stay reachable while on the move. Whether it is a quick reply, a short follow-up, or a fast check on the current status, Tammad keeps mobile access simple.

### Requirements

To use Tammad, you need:

- an existing Zammad instance
- a valid Zammad login

Tammad is an independent project and is not officially affiliated with Zammad GmbH.
